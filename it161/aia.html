Audience Information Information
Audiences Issues, Concerns, Needs Approach
Video Game Buyers
• Ages 15+
• Any gender
• Highly tech savvy
• Spend a lot of time online
• Prefer PC OS
• Prefer Chrome, FireFox, Opera browsers
• Varied resolution size
• Income varies with age
• Global
• Looking for accurate information about a game before purchasing
• Want to feel excited about the game
• Want to support an indie studio they like
• Need a path to a purchase page
• Create landing page for each game with exciting and informative marketing material (Eg: trailers, blurbs, FAQs.)
• Craft clear call-to-actions that link to a marketplace to purchase the game
• Provide an "About Us" and a developer's blog to humanize the studio
Fan Community
• Ages 15+, though skews younger than general customers
• Any gender, but may skew female and nonbinary
• Highly tech savvy
• Spend a lot of recreational time online, especially on social media platforms
• Generally prefer PC, but likely more Mac users than general customers
• Prefer Chrome, FireFox, Opera, and maybe Safari browsers
• Varied resolution size
• Income varies with age, but not as likely to be spending money since they probably already own the games
• Global
• May not actually play or buy the studio's games and instead just want to interact with the online fandom 		• Looking for pathways to interact with others in the fandom
• Want to feel supported by the studio
• Want to have opportunities to show off fan works
• Want to know more obscure information about the games, the development process, and the studio
• Need easy ways to stay in-the-loop on the studio's activity 		• Create fun pages on official lore and characters for each game
• Platform fan art (with the artist's permission)
• Give behind-the-scenes information such as cut content and development trivia via blogs and podcasts
• Host links to high-quality fan projects and communities, such as official subreddits
• Provide an opt-in email list to receive notifications like future releases
Games Media (Journalists, Streamers, etc.)
• Ages 18+, with rare exception for young influencers/streamers
• Any gender, but skews male
• Professionally tech savvy
• The Internet is a major part of their professional life
• Prefer PC; likely have powerful computing systems
• Prefer Chrome, Opera, browsers
• Varied resolution size
• Income varies wildly depending on career and context
• Global, but usually English-speaking 		• Looking for information and material useful for writing articles and creating content
• Looking for contact information for the studio itself • Want to feel that the studio has a positive relationship with media coverage and content creation
 • Create a press kit page to provide high-quality official assets for use in articles and content
• Provide clear contact information in the site's footer
• Host links to past articles and coverage
